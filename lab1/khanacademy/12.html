Recipe #1: Pasta

  • Time: 7-10 min
  • Serves: 1-3
Ingredients Quantity
pack of Pasta up to you
salt half of spoon
boiled water to cover pasta

Step 1: Put boiled water in pot.
Step 2: Put pasta in pot with water.
Step 3: Put salt in pot.
Step 4: Use small fire and wait for 7-10 minutes.
Step 5: The meal is done.

Recipe #3: Rice

  • Time: 7-10 min
  • Serves: 1-3
Ingredients Quantity
pack of Rice up to you
salt half of spoon
water a bit

Step 1: Put water in pot, so thatit can be just on surface of pot.
Step 2: Put rice in pot with water.
Step 3: Put salt in pot.
Step 4:Use small fire and wait for 20-25 minutes.
Step 5: The meal is done.
