Travel to Abu-Dhabi

Abu-Dhabi has many places to visit and see. It is a perfect place for tourists to rest and be fascinated by this sight seeings.

Places to visit

  • Ferrari World

    • It is the world's first Ferrari-themed park and features Formula Rossa, the world's fastest roller coaster.

  • Louvre Abu Dhabi

    • An art museum that runs under an agreement between the UAE and France, signed in March 2007, that allows it to use the Louvre's name until 2037, and has been described by the Louvre as "France's largest cultural project abroad."

  • Sea World

    • is a marine life theme park and animal researching, rescue & rehabilitation center.

Things to see

  • Sheikh Zayed Grand Mosque

    • It is the country's largest mosque, and is the key place of worship for daily Islamic prayers.

  • Qasr Al Hosn

    • It is a historical landmark and the oldest stone building in the city of Abu Dhabi"

  • Qasr Al Watan

    • The presidential palace of the United Arab Emirates.
